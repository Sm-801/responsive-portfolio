About Me

My name is Saul Macias and i'm originally from southern California. I graduated from Cal State Long Beach with a bachelor's degree in social sciences. Most of my professional career I have worked in the finance sector and most recently I have worked in the insurance industry. I have several years of managing sales teams in meeting and exceeding quotas and have lead teams in the regulatory side of insurance. I have project management experience and have overseen the development of systems to improve processes to increase productivity. Most recently I worked with databases in compiling client data for analytics which has given me the desire and need to learn more of the tech industry.

In my personal life the two titles that I enjoy most are those of husband and father. I have been married for 8 years and I have two kids, a 6 years old daughter and a 1 year old son. I am a Utah transplant and have enjoyed my years here in Utah and expect to raise my family here.

I have several hobbies which mostly include the outdoors. I love going on hikes and riding my mountain bike. I like to build things and fix things that are broken. I have always been interested in seeing how things are developed and built and I have developed a passion for coding. Being able to build things from nothing to something amazing is one of the reasons I enjoy programming.
